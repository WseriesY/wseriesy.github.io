## 爱好橱窗的主要内容 Contents of the Showcase

### 1. 语言学习与人造语言 Language Learning and Constructed Languages (Conlangs)

本人的语言学习成果包括自然语言及人造语言。

My language learning works contain natural languages and conlangs.

已学习的自然语言中有现今仍在使用的语言，如拉达克语；也有不再广泛使用的语言，如苏美尔语、于阗语等。

The natural languages I've learnt include some languages that are still in use, like Ladakhi, and some languages that are no longer used widely, like Sumerian, Khotanese, etc. 

已学习的人造语言中包含了国际辅助语，如道本语；以及艺术性人造语言，如阿尔伽语(Arka)。

The conlangs I've learnt include "International auxiliary languages" like Toki Pona, and artistic conlangs like Arka.

本人还自己创作了一些人造语言。我创造的一般是以纪念喜欢的人物形象为目的，在语系层面少量参考了现实原型制作的“近架空语言”，如千织语。

Moreover, I've created some handmade conlangs. What I create are generally "penconlangs", like Chiorian, for the purpose of commemorating the figures I like, and that have consulted some language families in the real world.

### 2. 架空世界 Fantasy Worlds/ Conworlds

包括但不限于自创的架空世界和受许可的对他人创作的架空世界的研究。目前仍在进行中。

The contents include the handmade conworld and the permitted researches of other conworlds. In process now.

### 3. ACG的架空二创 ACG Constructed Secondary Creations

指受许可的对知名ACG架空世界的二次创作。本人最得意的作品即是“现代化提瓦特设定”。

These refer to my permitted Secondary Creations of some famous ACG conworlds. My most pleasant work is "the Modern Teyvat Settings".
